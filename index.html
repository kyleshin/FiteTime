About Fite Time


Fite Time is a MMA app that provides schedules and results for popular MMA events, such as UFC and Bellator. Most events are updated live, keeping you informed on the progress of the fight card. Fite Time also provides useful information such as broadcast channel and time(US only) for various portions of the event.


FEATURES

RESULTS

Vote on whom you think would win a fight, and see how others voted.

W-L-D and tale of the tape for fights.
